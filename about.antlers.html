What we do

The Circle is a bold initiative by Ugail Foundation to empower people across the spectrum of all ages and abilities. Through the Circle, we provide modern and comfortable spaces in which intellectual stimulation thrives - these are places people can carry out intellectual work. With our programme, we create novel learning initiatives for people to reach their full potential. Furthermore, we help people to carry out curiousity-driven innovation through which they enhance their own lives and make the world a better place.

Spaces

Dedicated places where likeminded individuals can meet, read, learn, and work together. These are spacious environments with the right amount of ambient to make reading, learning, and working even more enjoyable.

Academy

Delivering novel science, technology, engineering, art, and mathematics (STEAM) based educational programmes for people of all ages.

Catalyst

Through catalyst programmes, we have individuals or groups take an innovative seed idea and help develop it into a fully-fledged business.
